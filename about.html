About Alph Teach Auto & Towing Services

8 Years of Keeping Johannesburg Moving

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Alph Teach Auto & Towing Services began as a family-owned towing operation with a single truck and a strong belief in service excellence. From the first call, our goal has been to offer Johannesburg dependable roadside support and workshop repair with honesty, transparency, and care.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted company serving Wemmer and surrounding areas. Today, we operate a modern fleet of tow trucks and a fully equipped workshop at Commercial Park, delivering fast responses and skilled auto service on every job.

Despite our growth, one thing remains constant: we treat every customer and every vehicle as if it were our own. That means clear pricing, reliable communication, and a commitment to help whenever you need us most.

Our Mission

To deliver fast, reliable and affordable towing and auto repair services across Johannesburg. We work to reduce the stress of vehicle emergencies by providing professional support, honest advice, and a personal approach to every service.

  • Responding to towing calls within 30 minutes
  • Providing honest diagnostics and repair recommendations
  • Offering competitive, upfront pricing
  • Treating every vehicle with care and respect

Our Vision

To become Johannesburg's most trusted towing and auto repair partner. We are focused on growth through exceptional service, continuous improvement and a reputation built on integrity, safety and availability 24/7.
