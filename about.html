About Alph Teach Auto & Towing Services

8 Years of Keeping Johannesburg Moving

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Alph Teach Auto & Towing Services started as a small, family-owned towing operation with just one truck and a dream. Our founder, Alph Teach, recognized a gap in the market for reliable, honest, and affordable towing and auto repair services in the Johannesburg area.

From those humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name in Wemmer and beyond. Today, we operate a fleet of modern tow trucks and a fully equipped auto repair workshop at our Commercial Park location.

What hasn't changed? Our commitment to treating every customer like family, providing transparent pricing with no hidden fees, and being there when you need us most — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable towing and auto repair services to the Johannesburg community. We strive to reduce the stress of vehicle emergencies by delivering professional service with a personal touch, every time.

  • Responding to towing calls within 30 minutes
  • Providing honest diagnostics and repair recommendations
  • Offering competitive, upfront pricing
  • Treating every vehicle with care and respect

Our Vision

To become Johannesburg's most trusted name in towing and auto repair, known for excellence, integrity, and 24/7 reliability. We aim to expand our fleet and workshop capabilities while maintaining the personal service that built our reputation.

What We Stand For
